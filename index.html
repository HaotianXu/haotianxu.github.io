About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics and Statistics at Auburn University. Previously, I was a Harrison Early Career Assistant Professor in the Department of Statistics at the University of Warwick. I have also held postdoctoral research positions at the Pennsylvania State University with Prof. Runze Li and at the University of Warwick with Prof. Yi Yu. I earned my Ph.D. in Statistics from the University of Geneva under the supervision of Prof. Maria-Pia Victoria-Feser and Prof. Stéphane Guerrier, and I hold an M.Sc. in Statistics from the University of Illinois at Urbana–Champaign.

Research Interests

  • Nonparametric estimation in high-dimensions.
  • Change point detection and inference.
  • High-dimensional inference under temporal dependence and contamination.
